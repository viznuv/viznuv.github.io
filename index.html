About

Background

I am an Assistant Professor of Marketing at Jio Institute, working at the intersection of artificial intelligence, consumer behaviour, and social networks. My work investigates how emerging technologies - AI, blockchain, and metaverse platforms - reshape consumer decision-making and brand–consumer relationships. I employ experimental and computational methods to integrate behavioural theory with data-driven approaches.

I also spearheaded the curriculum and planning for Jio's AI Classroom initiative, which has reached more than 600,000 learners under the "AI for All" concept. Prior to academia, I served as a Product Manager at Vodafone India.
