Research Philosophy

Four Pillars

Evidence-Based Managerial Science

Integration of behavioural theory with computational rigour to produce findings that are both scientifically defensible and managerially actionable.

Interdisciplinary Methodological Fusion

Bridging marketing, information systems, and computer science - combining the explanatory depth of social-science theory with the predictive power of computational methods.

Multi-Methodological Triangulation

Cross-validating insights through experimental, observational, and computational methods so that conclusions are robust to method-specific limitations.

Ethical AI & Societal Responsibility

Embedding ethics and impact assessment into algorithmic design - recognising that the systems we build shape the markets and societies they serve.
